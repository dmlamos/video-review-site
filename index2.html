Attorney Profile

David M. Lamos is a Florida attorney with decades of experience in criminal defense, appellate, and post-conviction litigation.

His practice is focused on careful examination of the State’s evidence, identification of legal and procedural issues reflected in the record, and disciplined enforcement of constitutional safeguards. Representation is grounded in analysis and preparation, not assumptions or promises.

Post-Conviction Review: Common Grounds

  • Denial of the right to appeal
  • Lack of jurisdiction to enter judgment or impose sentence
  • Sentences exceeding the maximum authorized by law
  • Ineffective assistance of counsel, including attorney error or mishandling reflected in the record
  • Newly discovered evidence
  • Changes in the law that apply retroactively

Each case depends on the record, the governing law, and the procedural posture of the matter.

Video Evidence Review

Video evidence is present in many criminal cases, but video does not always confirm what is claimed.

  • What the video clearly shows
  • What the video does not show
  • Gaps, inconsistencies, or ambiguities
  • Differences between video evidence and written reports
  • Timing, perspective, and visibility limitations

This service is analytical in nature and does not predict outcomes.
