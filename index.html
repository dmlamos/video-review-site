Public Record Assistance

Informational
Arrest-Record Review

Many arrests, including arrests handled in St. Lucie County, involve recorded evidence such as body-worn camera footage, patrol vehicle video, jail intake recordings, or other surveillance. These recordings are public records but are often held by different agencies.

This page allows you to request an optional, informational review of an arrest affidavit for the limited purpose of identifying whether recorded evidence likely exists and which agencies typically control those recordings.
